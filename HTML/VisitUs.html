Hello, Traveler!

In the current health context, we request that visitors practice social distancing and use the hand sanitizer dispensers at the entrance of the museum. We recommend wearing a mask during the duration of your visit. You can buy your tickets directly at the museum or on site. For your information:
1. The reception counters are equipped with glass walls,
2. The audioguides are disinfected after each use, Lifts, which are regularly disinfected, are reserved for people with reduced mobility,
3. Changing rooms and lockers are closed. Please do not bring coats nor backpacks.

Access

1. Most of the rooms offer wheelchair access (ground floor, Salle Monet, temporary exhibition). The permanent exhibition rooms on the first floor can be reached by lift (standing).
2. Wheelchairs are available at reception.
3. Disabled parking is provided outside the Museum as well as space for disabled access minibuses.
4. Pushchairs are allowed in the Museum.

To Discover At The Museum: MYSE

What is Myse?

Myse is a mobile application that enables visitors to museums, exhibitions and galleries to create their own personalized catalog throughout their visit, selecting only the works that interest them. The user can also choose the format and cover of his book, and add a personalized photo and caption, which will allow him to leave with a one-of-a-kind copy.
The Musée Marmottan Monet is the app’s first partner.
Downloadable from Apple Store and Google Play.
For further information, go directly to: "https://myse.museum/"
